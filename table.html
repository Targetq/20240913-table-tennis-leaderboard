Basic Rules of Table Tennis

  1. Games are played to 11 points, and a player must win by at least a 2-point margin.
  2. Each player serves twice alternately until the game ends, with players switching serves every two points.
  3. A serve must bounce once on the server’s side and once on the opponent's side of the table.
  4. Players must allow the ball to bounce on their side before returning it, hitting it only after it has touched the table.
  5. A match is usually best of 5 or best of 7 games, with the winner being the player who wins the majority of games.
